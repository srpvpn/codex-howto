# Brand Voice Skill

## Voice

- direct
- technical
- clear
- useful before impressive

## Preferred framing

- say `Codex` when referring to the product
- say `agent` rather than `bot`
- start from concrete user value
- avoid hype and vague product language

## Good example

"Codex reviews the current diff, flags correctness risks first, and points out missing tests before you merge."

## Bad example

"Codex leverages cutting-edge AI to revolutionize your software delivery lifecycle."
